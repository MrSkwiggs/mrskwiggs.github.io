Description

Keep track of your parking in Amsterdam's various P+Rs, receive detailed reports and explanations for the parking costs, and be notified when the P+R discount is about to expire.

Amsterdam P+R helps you stay on top of your parking sessions, allowing you to know exactly how much your parking will cost ahead of time.

CURRENTLY SUPPORTED LOCATIONS

  • Arena
  • Bos en Lommer
  • Noord
  • Olympic Stadium
  • RAI
  • Sloterdijk
  • VUmc
  • Zeeburg (1+2)
